About Sam

Coaching Backed by Science. Driven by Results.

Hi, I'm Sam — a personal trainer based in Bishops Stortford with a passion for helping ordinary people achieve extraordinary results.

A proud graduate of Nottingham Trent University's BSc Sport & Exercise Science programme — one of the most respected in the UK — Sam's approach to coaching is grounded in real sports science, not guesswork. During his time at NTU, Sam didn't just sit in lecture halls: he was hands-on every single day. He worked directly with NTU's competitive university sports teams, assisted elite-level coaches on performance placements, and clocked hundreds of hours of practical coaching experience before most trainers had even qualified.

That rare blend of academic excellence and real-world application means Sam genuinely understands why the body responds the way it does — and he uses that knowledge to build programmes that are smarter, more effective, and more sustainable than anything you'll find elsewhere.

Since graduating, Sam has gone on to become one of the most sought-after coaches in Hertfordshire — transforming over 200 clients across all walks of life: busy parents who've reclaimed their bodies, professionals who've shed years of desk weight, athletes who've broken personal records, and complete beginners who've discovered a love for fitness they never thought possible. Every single programme Sam writes is built from scratch, tailored entirely to you — your goals, your schedule, your life.

Based right here in Bishops Stortford, Sam trains clients in-person across Hertfordshire and offers fully managed online coaching for those anywhere in the world. Whatever your goal — he will get you there.
