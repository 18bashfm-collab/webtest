About Sam

Coaching You Can Actually Trust

Hi, I'm Sam — a personal trainer based in Bishops Stortford who believes fitness should fit your life, not the other way around.

With over 8 years of experience helping everyday people — busy parents, office workers, athletes, and everyone in between — build stronger, leaner, healthier bodies, I know that cookie-cutter programmes don't cut it. Every plan I write is built from scratch around you: your schedule, your goals, your starting point.

I train clients in-person across Bishops Stortford and the surrounding Hertfordshire area, and I offer fully remote online coaching for those further afield. Whether you want to lose fat, build muscle, compete in a sport, or simply feel better in your own skin — let's make it happen.
